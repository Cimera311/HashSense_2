How GMT Maintenance Discount Works

By holding or locking GMT tokens on your wallet, you can reduce your daily maintenance costs by up to 20%.

  • Maximum Discount: 20% at 378 days of maintenance coverage
  • No Discount: Less than 36 days = 0%
  • Linear Scaling: 36 days = 1%, 54 days = 2%, 72 days = 3%, etc.
  • Coverage Days: GMT Value / Daily Maintenance Cost
